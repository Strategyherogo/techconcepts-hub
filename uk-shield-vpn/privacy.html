No-Logs Policy

We do not collect, store, or monitor:

  • Your browsing history or websites visited
  • Traffic destinations or content

  • Connection timestamps or session duration

  • Any metadata that could identify your activity

Data Security & Encryption

Technical Security Measures:

  • AES-256 encryption for all VPN connections
  • TLS 1.3 for API communications
  • Secure key management and rotation
  • WebRTC leak protection
  • DNS leak protection
  • Kill switch functionality

Chrome Extension Permissions

Our Chrome extension requires specific permissions to function:

  • Proxy: To route your traffic through our VPN servers
  • WebRequest: To implement kill switch and leak protection
  • Storage: To save your preferences locally
  • Privacy: To configure browser privacy settings
  • All URLs: To protect all your web traffic

These permissions are essential for VPN functionality and are not used for data collection.
