Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

VC Deal Analyzer collects minimal information necessary to provide our investment analysis service:

  • Usage Data: Number of analyses performed, feature usage statistics
  • Company Data: Publicly available company information from websites you visit
  • Technical Data: Browser type, extension version for troubleshooting

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered investment analysis
  • Track usage limits for free and paid tiers
  • Improve our analysis algorithms
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Analysis results are stored locally in your browser
  • Usage tracking data is stored securely on our servers
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

4. Data Retention

  • Local analysis data: Stored until you clear browser data
  • Usage tracking: Retained for 12 months for billing purposes
  • Account data: Retained while your subscription is active

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of data collection by uninstalling the extension

6. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy)
  • AI Services: For generating investment analysis (data is not stored by AI providers)

7. Cookies and Local Storage

The extension uses browser local storage to save your preferences and analysis history. No tracking cookies are used.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the extension or our website.

9. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements including:

  • Lawful basis for processing (legitimate interest for service provision)
  • Data minimization (we collect only necessary data)
  • User rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
  • Data protection by design and default
